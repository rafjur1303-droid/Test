RESEARCH SPEED INFO

You can buy research speed by

-Money (green number)

-Multiplier (red number)

-Rebirth (blue number)

-Super rebirth (light blue number)

Your total research speed is equal to

Money speed * Multiplier speed * Rebirth speed * Super rebirth speed * Upgrade speed

Upgrade speed will increase when you do some research

RESEARCH SPEEDING INFO

You can assign your researchers to do research (gray buttons)

To change number of researchers that goes to research you must click on right most panel

How it works?

-First panel shows you how many researchers you have (current researchers / all yours researchers)

-Second panel shows your research speed

-Third one is a button that will recall all researchers from research so you don't have to do it manually

-Fourth panel shows you how many researchers you assign to research

Next thing is a 3x3 square made from buttons ands it works like this:

-First column (three buttons) will just increase research spending by 1 , 10 or 100

-Second column (three buttons) is similar to first one , but it decreases research spending by 1 , 10 or 100

-Last column (three buttons) will calculate your researchers percent for 40 researchers 10%-4 , 25%-10 , 100%-40

And last button will reset how many you assign

AUTOMATION INFO

Automation work like this:

You have one panel and two buttons

Panel shows you how much % of current gain and how long it takes

At first button you can increase % of gain , and it shows how much % you will get at next lv

At second button you can decrease how long you need to wait and also shows time at next lv
